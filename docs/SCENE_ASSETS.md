# v0.4.0 场景素材记录

生成方式：Codex 内置 `image_gen`。三个场景均以 `public/scenes/coast-afternoon-v1.webp` 作为构图、机位、前景预留和质量参考，不是编辑原图。

## 林间晴窗

最终文件：`public/scenes/forest-window-v1.webp`

```text
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: full-viewport website environment background for the same browser piano product
Input images: Image 1 is the composition, camera height, foreground reserve, and quality reference only.
Primary request: create a new original photorealistic scene named “林间晴窗”, a peaceful sunlit forest music room viewed from the seated pianist’s eye level.
Scene/backdrop: a wide open window facing a lush spring forest, soft moss, slender trees, a small clear stream in the middle distance, white sheer curtains at the sides, restrained plants and a ceramic cup on the sill.
Composition/framing: preserve Image 1’s wide landscape framing and horizon placement; keep the central vertical area uncluttered for music particles; reserve the bottom 28% as a dark warm walnut piano fallboard/foreground surface with no keys so an HTML piano keyboard can be overlaid.
Lighting/mood: bright late-morning woodland sunlight, gentle leaf-filtered rays, airy, restorative and calm, noticeably different from the seaside scene.
Color palette: fresh leaf green, warm cream, moss, soft amber sunlight, dark walnut.
Materials/textures: natural wood grain, translucent linen curtains, realistic foliage and water.
Constraints: no piano keys, no instrument, no people, no user interface, no text, no logos, no watermark; important objects away from center and bottom keyboard area.
Avoid: fantasy forest, neon, excessive fog, oversaturated green, clutter, warped windows, duplicated objects.
```
